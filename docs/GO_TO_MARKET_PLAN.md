# Go-To-Market Plan: $50K–$100K in 4 Months

## The Product You're Selling

**WhatsApp AI Sales Concierge** — An AI employee that answers leads on WhatsApp 24/7, qualifies them, collects their info, and books appointments. Agents only talk to pre-qualified, booked leads.

**Core value proposition (one sentence):**
> "Never lose a WhatsApp lead again — our AI responds in seconds, qualifies buyers, and books viewings while you sleep."

---

## 1. Target Market (Where the Money Is)

### Primary: UAE Real Estate Agents & Brokerages
**Why this is the #1 market:**
- **WhatsApp is king** — 95%+ of customer-agent communication in UAE is WhatsApp
- **High transaction value** — Average Dubai property sale = $300K-$2M → agents earn 2% commission ($6K-$40K per deal)
- **Lead response time = money** — Studies show leads contacted within 5 min are 21x more likely to convert. Most agents take hours to reply.
- **Pain point is massive** — Agents juggle 50-200 WhatsApp leads/month manually. They lose 40-60% of leads to slow response.
- **They can afford it** — A single closed deal pays for years of your service
- **Large market** — Dubai alone has 30,000+ registered real estate agents, ~5,000 active brokerages

### Secondary Markets (Month 3-4 expansion):
- **UAE Automotive dealers** — Same dynamics, high-value transactions
- **UAE Medical clinics** — High volume, appointment-driven
- **UAE Home services** — HVAC, cleaning, maintenance companies
- **India real estate** (Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi NCR) — Huge WhatsApp market

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):
- Real estate agent or small brokerage (2-20 agents)
- Gets 20+ WhatsApp inquiries per month
- Already runs Facebook/Instagram/Google ads
- Frustrated with slow lead response and missed opportunities
- Based in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or Sharjah
- Budget: $500-2000/month (trivial compared to ad spend)

## 2. Pricing Strategy

### Tier Structure

| Plan | Monthly | Conversations | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free Trial** | $0 (7 days) | 25 | Try before you buy |
| **Starter** | $197/mo | 200/mo | Solo agents |
| **Growth** | $497/mo | 800/mo | Growing teams |
| **Scale** | $997/mo | 2,500/mo | Brokerages & teams |

**No setup fees.** Free onboarding call included.

### Why These Prices Work
- **ROI is obvious**: If the AI helps close just 1 extra deal per month ($6K+ commission), the agent makes 6-30x their investment
- **No setup fee = low friction**: Agents can start immediately without upfront commitment
- **Monthly is sticky**: Once leads are flowing through the system, agents won't cancel
- **Competitors charge more**: Salesforce, HubSpot, etc. cost $150-300/user/month with NO WhatsApp AI

### Revenue Math to Hit $50K-$100K

**Conservative ($50K in 4 months):**
```
Month 1: 8 Starter ($197) + 2 Growth ($497) = $2,570/mo
Month 2: 15 Starter + 5 Growth    = $5,440/mo (cumulative)
Month 3: 22 Starter + 10 Growth   = $9,304/mo
Month 4: 28 Starter + 15 Growth   = $12,971/mo
4-month total MRR sum ≈ $30,285 + upsells to Scale ($997) ≈ $50K
```

**Aggressive ($100K in 4 months):**

## 4. Sales Playbook

### The Pitch (30 seconds)

> "You know how you lose leads because you can't reply to WhatsApp fast enough? Our AI responds in 5 seconds, asks the right qualifying questions, collects their name and email, and books a viewing on your calendar — all automatically. You just show up to the viewing. Want to see it work with your actual WhatsApp number?"

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I don't trust AI talking to my clients" | "That's why we built the takeover button. You see every message in real-time. One click and you're in the conversation. The AI is your first responder, not your replacement." |
| "It's too expensive" | "How much is one closed deal worth to you? $6,000? $20,000? If our AI helps you close just one extra deal this month, you've made 10x your investment." |
| "My leads are different/special" | "We customize the AI for your exact business — your areas, your price ranges, your style. It's trained on your services, not generic scripts." |
| "I want to try it first" | "Absolutely. 7-day free trial on your real number. If you don't see results, you pay nothing." |
| "I'll think about it" | "While you think about it, how many WhatsApp leads came in today that you haven't replied to yet? Every hour you wait, those leads go cold." |

### Demo Script (5 minutes)
1. **Show the problem** (30 sec): "Check your WhatsApp right now. How many unread messages from potential clients?"
2. **Live demo** (2 min): Send a WhatsApp message to the demo number. Watch the AI respond, qualify, and book.
3. **Dashboard** (1 min): Show the lead in the dashboard with all info collected, appointment booked.
4. **Takeover** (30 sec): Show the human handoff toggle — "You're always in control."
5. **Close** (1 min): "Shall we set this up on your number? Takes 30 minutes."

## 6. What NOT to Build (Critical)

To hit $50-100K in 4 months, you need to **sell, not build**. The product is 90% ready.

**Do NOT build:**
- ❌ Property listings system (agents have their own)
- ❌ CRM features (they use their existing CRM)
- ❌ Email marketing (not your product)
- ❌ Website builder
- ❌ Advanced analytics dashboards
- ❌ Multi-language UI (English dashboard is fine, AI already speaks Arabic)

**DO build (only if clients ask):**
- ✅ A simple media/link sharing tool (agent pastes a URL, AI sends it)
- ✅ Better Arabic AI responses (if UAE clients struggle with Arabic quality)
- ✅ WhatsApp template messages for follow-ups (Twilio supports these)
- ✅ Simple daily summary email to agents ("You got 8 new leads today, 3 viewings booked")

## 9. Risks & Mitigation

| Slow sales cycle | Medium | Free trial removes risk for buyer. Demo is live, not slides. |
